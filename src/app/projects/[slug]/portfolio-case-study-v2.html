About the project

Premium Greek yogurt entering the European market

Our Task

Brand identity, naming, packaging design, art direction for a premium Greek yogurt line.

Client Goal

Position the product as authentic, high-quality Greek import — distinguished from mass-market competitors through design that communicates heritage and craft.

Disciplines

Brand Strategy Naming Visual Identity Packaging Art Direction

Noble heritage — referencing European ceramic and cartographic traditions to signal provenance and craft.

Name

Provenance — too literal, lacked shelf presence.

Packaging

Typography

The primary typeface is a transitional serif, chosen for its historical resonance with 18th-century European printing — connecting the visual system to the product's heritage. Secondary type is a restrained geometric sans for functional information and ingredient lists.
